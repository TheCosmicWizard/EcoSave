About US

At EcoSave, our mission is simple – make waste management smarter, greener, and easier for everyone. We believe that small, everyday actions can create a big impact on the environment. That's why we built EcoSave, an AI-powered waste recognition system that helps individuals and communities identify waste categories in seconds. Whether it's recyclable plastics, compostable organics, or non-recyclables, EcoSave guides you to dispose responsibly – reducing landfill waste and promoting sustainable living.

Our Vision:

  • Empower people to make eco-friendly disposal decisions.
  • Promote a cleaner, greener future through AI-driven solutions.
  • Reduce the carbon footprint by encouraging sustainable habits.

Together, we can create a world where waste becomes a resource, not a problem.
